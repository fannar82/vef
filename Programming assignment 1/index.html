=======
  • When text is selected, thickness is multiplied by 5 and used as font size.
  • Images are not affected by thickness or color.
  • To select an item, click Ctrl and select items with left mouse button.
  • To move selected items, hold Shift and move mouse (no mouse down required)
  • When save is clicked, the properties of the image are sent to the textbox under the canvas. That text can be saved on a hard drive with a texteditor. Only the current whiteboard is saved.
  • To load an older drawing. Enter the text into the textbox under the canvas and hit load. Only the current whiteboard is affected and anything on the current whiteboard is cleared.
